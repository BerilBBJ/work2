This is my second 13-inch MacBook Pro in an aluminum case, the first still works although it was produced in mid-2010. The moral obsolescence of the hardware and software of the existing computer made it necessary to purchase a modern and productive laptop to replace the outdated one. There is a huge chasm between the MacBook Pro 13 mid-2010 and the MacBook Pro 13 mid-2020, not only technically, but also in the very philosophy of technology from Apple. The new model is more compact and lightweight, but it doesn't have that unique Apple temperament that comes with the model's container. This is a magnetic charge, a lamp flashing in time with human breathing in the right corner of the front of the case and a glowing apple. It even upset me a little because you get used to technology, and Apple technology is always something personal. The computer is quite productive with a gorgeous screen, on which it is impossible to see the pixels, since Retina technology and True Tone auto-backlight adapts to light and changes color temperature. Suitable for watching video perfectly, video in 2K pulls without straining. The number of USB-C connectors is sufficient and the power cable can be connected from either side of the laptop. The kit comes with a long 2 meter cable, for me it's even a lot. It copes well with processing 20-30 megabit uncompressed photos, there is enough power, the laptop heats up slightly. Of course, the noise of coolers is not heard, everything is very quiet during operation. The trackpad is large and easy to use, there is no desire to buy an additional mouse or something else. It is convenient to control the cursor, all sorts of gestures and swipes help you in your work. Soft recoil from the vibrator Taptik Engin as the trackpad is made of glass and there is no mechanical vibration in it. It is worth noting the high-quality sound of the laptop through the built-in speakers. I don't know how the engineers managed to squeeze out such a confused and clear sound from this kid. I am satisfied with the purchase, I hope it will please me for at least ten years.
Thanks to the seller for the low price. I saved a lot of money
